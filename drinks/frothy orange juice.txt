Frothy Orange Juice
=================================================
Source: Breville Juicer manual
Servings/Yield: 3
Prep Time: 5 minutes

=================================================
Ingredients

35 ounces oranges, peeled



=================================================
Directions

Process oranges through juicer. Serve immediately.


=================================================
Notes

Oranges should be refrigerated before juicing.
