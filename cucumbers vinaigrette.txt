Source: Moosewood Restaurant
Servings/Yield: 4
Prep Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients: 
	2 cucumber, peeled
	1/4 tablespoon vinegar
	2 tablespoons sugar
	1/2 teaspoon salt
	1/2 teaspoon mustard
	, ground, dried
	pepper, to taste

Directions: 
	Slice the cucumbers crosswise into 1/8 to 1/4-inch rounds. Combine the vinegar, sugar, salt, and mustard in a serving bowl. Toss with the sliced cucumbers and add pepper to taste. Serve immediately or refrigerate until ready to use.
