Servings/Yield: 4 servings
Prep Time: 

Ingredients: 
	60ml hoisin sauce
	120ml passata (bottled strained potatoes)
	4 scallions, thinly sliced
	4cm piece fresh root ginger, peeled and finely chopped
	2 fresh red chiles, seeded and cut into fine strips
	2 garlic cloves, crushed
	few drops of roasted sesame oil
	
Directions: 
	Mix the hoisin sauce and passata into a bowl. Stir in the scallions, ginger, chiles, and crushed garlic. Add the sesame oil , mix well and serve.
