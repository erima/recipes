Source: Moosewood Restaurant
Servings/Yield: 6
Prep Time: 

Ingredients: 
	2 cups onions, chopped
	1 garlic clove, minced
	3 cups potatoes, diced
	1 cup chopped, celery
	5 cups water
	1 cup carrots, peeled and chopped
	1 cauliflower, chopped, about 5 cups
	1 heaping tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
	1 tablespoon lemon juice
	1 teaspoon ground dried mustard
	1/4 teaspoon caraway seeds
	2 cups buttermilk
	salt, to taste
	chopped scallions

Directions: 
	In a soup pot combine the onions, garlic, potatoes, celery, and water. Bring to a boil, reduce the heat, and then simmer for 10 minutes. Add the carrots and continue to simmer for 10 more minutes. Add the cauliflower, dill, lemon juice, mustard, and caraway seeds and simmer for 15 to 20 minutes longer, until the carrots are tender. In a blender or food processor, pur√©e the soup with the buttermilk, working in batches. Add salt to taste. Gently reheat.
	
	Serve topped with chopped scallions
