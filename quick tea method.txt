1. Grab 1 1/2 teaspoons of tea, drop into an empty teabag or strainer, put into mug.
2. Generally speaking, fill the mug with the hottest water you can find (if your brewing green tea, mix in a little cool water).
3. Steep for 3 minutes, then discard tea leaves and you’re good to-go.
