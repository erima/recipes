Source: 400 Wok & Stir–Fry Recipes
Servings/Yield: 1 small jar
Prep Time: 

Ingredients: 
	90ml tamarind paste
	90ml water
	45ml caster (superfine) sugar
	
Directions: 
	Place all the ingredients in a small pan and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and cook gently for 3–4 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove the pan from the heat and transfer to a small bowl. Leave to cool before serving.
