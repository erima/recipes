Source: Whole Foods
Servings/Yield: 6
Prep Time: 

Ingredients:
1 cup Jasmine rice
1 tablespoon olive oil
1 to taste salt
1 small onion, finely chopped
pepper
2 1/2 cups small organic broccoli florets
15 ounces creamy mushroom soup, organic
1 cup cheddar cheese, shredded


Directions:
Spray a 7x11-inch baking dish with olive oil cooking spray and set aside.

Cook rice according to package instructions adding 1/2 tablespoon olive oil and 1/2 teaspoon sea salt.

While rice is cooking, preheat oven to 350ºF. Heat the remaining 1/2 tablespoon of olive oil in a sauté pan over medium heat. Sauté onions for 5 minutes, stirring often. Add broccoli florets and sauté for another 3 minutes, stirring often.

Add cooked rice to onion and broccoli. Stir in the soup and 1/2 cup of the cheese. Taste and adjust seasonings, adding black pepper.

Transfer to the prepared baking dish and sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup cheese. Bake for 1/2 hour or until cheese is browned and casserole is bubbly.

Notes: 
Per serving (155g-wt.): 230 calories (120 from fat), 13g total fat, 5g saturated fat, 8g protein, 20g total carbohydrate (1g dietary fiber, 2g sugar), 20mg cholesterol, 680mg sodium
