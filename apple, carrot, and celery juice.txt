Source: Breville Manual 
Servings/Yield: 2
Prep Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:
4 small granny smith apple
3 medium carrots, peeled
4 sticks celery

Directions:
Process apples, carrots, and celery through juicer. Mix well and serve immediately. 

Notes: 
Apples vs. carrots can be tweaked for sweetness.
