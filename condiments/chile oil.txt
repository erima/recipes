Source: Whole Foods
Servings/Yield: 3 (8-ounce) bottles of oil
Prep Time: 

Ingredients: 
	3 teaspoons peppercorns (white, black or pink) 
	3 teaspoons cumin seeds 
	3 teaspoons red chile flakes 
	1 1/2 teaspoons salt 
	1 (25.4-ounce) bottle extra virgin olive oil or canola oil 
	10 whole dried chile pods 

Directions: 
	Wash 3 glass bottles with hot soapy water, rinse well and set aside to dry. Once dry, place 1 teaspoon peppercorns, 1 teaspoon cumin, 1 teaspoon red chile flakes and 1/2 teaspoon salt in each bottle. Heat olive oil and chile pods in a sauce pan to 150Â°F, turn heat off and allow oil to sit for a half hour. Carefully strain out chile pods. Place funnel in the bottle opening and pour oil in the bottle, leaving about 1 to 2 inches at the top. Shake to mix well, seal and store in a cool dark place for 1 week before enjoying. 

	
Notes: 
	Package with a stainless steel spout around the neck of the bottle for easy dispensing.
