Source: Tea Zone
Servings: 1
Prep Time:

Ingredients:
4 grams Matcha
2 tablespoons sugar
3 tablespoons hot water
10 ounces milk
ice

Directions:
1) Blend dry Matcha and sugar in a glass. 
2) Pour in the hot water and whisk into a paste. 
3) Pour cold milk over the Matcha mixture. 
4) Add ice.
