Source: Bob's Red Mill
Servings/Yield: 3
Prep Time: 

Ingredients: 
	3 cups water
	3/8 teaspoon salt
	3/4 cups cream of wheat
	  
	2 1/4 teaspoons butter, optional
	maple syrup, optional
	cinnamon, optional

Directions: 
	Bring water and salt to a near boil. Remove from heat, slowly add cream of wheat and return to burner. Bring to a boil again then lower heat and cook approximately 3 minutes or until thickened, stirring occasionally.

Notes: 
	Butter seems to be a good addition to add some flavor.
