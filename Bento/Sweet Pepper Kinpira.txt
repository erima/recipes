Sweet Pepper Kinpira
=============================
Source: Just Bento
Servings/Yield: 1 serving
Prep Time: 
Type: Vegetable
Prepare: Frozen or up to 1 week before

=============================
Ingredients

2 teaspoons sesame oil
1/2 each yellow and red sweet pepper, de-seeded and sliced
1 tablespoon white sesame seeds, toasted
1 tablespoon soy sauce
Pinch red chili flakes

=============================
Directions

Heat the oil in a frying pan over high heat and add the sweet peppers. Saut√© for 3 - 4 minutes until they turn a brighter color but are still crisp. Add the sesame seeds, soy sauce, and chili. Let cool before packing.

Make this Kinpira using two whole sweet peppers and doubling the other ingredients. It will keep for up to a week in the refrigerator.

=============================
Notes

Should be sliced into strips like French fries.
