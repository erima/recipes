Instant Cabbage and Cucumber Pickles
=============================
Source: Just Bento
Servings/Yield: 1
Prep Time: 
Type: Vegetable
Prepare: 

=============================
Ingredients

1 large green cabbage leaf
2 inch (5cm) length English cucumber, sliced into thin rounds.
1/2 teaspoon sea salt
Squeeze lemon juice

=============================
Directions

Cut out the tough vein of the cabbage leaf, and slice the rest into strips. Sprinkle the cabbage and cucumber with the salt, and massage well with your hands until the vegetables go limp. Let rest for at least five minutes. Add a squeeze of lemon juice. 

Squeeze out any excess moisture before packing into a bento box. 

=============================
