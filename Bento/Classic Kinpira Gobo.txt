Classic Kinpira Gobo
=============================
Source: Just Bento
Servings/Yield: 4-6 servings
Prep Time: 
Type: Vegetable
Prepare: Frozen, or 1 week before

=============================
Ingredients

1 burdock root
1 tablespoon vegetable oil
2 teaspoons sesame oil
1/2 cup (120ml) dashi stock
1 tablespoon sugar
3 tablespoons soy sauce
2 tablespoons white sesame seeds, toasted
Shichimi pepper, to taste

=============================
Directions

Peel the burdock root (cutting it into sections first helps) and cut into thin slices on the diagonal. Cut the slices into matchsticks. Put te matchsticks into a bowl of water ad leave for 15 minutes.

Heat the oil in a frying pan or wok over high heat. Put in the well-drained burdock and stir-fry briefly. Add the dashi, sugar, and soy sauce and cook rapidly until the liquid has evaporated. Add the sesame seeds and stir until they pop. Sprinkle with shichimi pepper to taste.

=============================
Notes

For another classic, try taking the above and substituting carrot for half or all of burdock root. Omit sugar.
