Source:  Erik, liberally stolen from Jules Catering
Servings/Yield: 4
Prep Time: 

Ingredients: 
	3/4 cup vegenaise
	1 lb. pasta
	3/4 cup grape tomatoes, halved
	6 ounces artichoke hearts, with oil
	1 teaspoon black pepper
	1 teaspoon salt
	1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
	1/2 teaspoon dry mustard
	4 ounces sun dried tomatoes
	1/4 cup red onion
	1/2 cup parmesan cheese


Directions: 
	Cook pasta per instructions.

	Combine onion, grape tomatoes, artichoke hearts, and sun dried tomatoes in a large bowl. In a separate bowl combine vegenaise, pepper, cayenne, salt and mustard. Stir vegenaise mixture in with ingredients in the large bowl. Slowly stir in pasta.

	Refrigerate overnight.
