Source: Breville Manual
Servings/Yield: 2
Prep Time: 10 minutes

Ingredients:
1 small granny smith apple
3 small pears, ripe
1 cup  strawberries, cleaned

Directions:
Process apple, pears, and strawberries through Juice Fountain. Serve Immediately.
