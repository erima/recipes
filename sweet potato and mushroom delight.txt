Source: Alissa Mallinson Prep time: 10 minutesCook time: 20 minutesIngredients:1/4 onion, diced1 sweet potato, peeled and cubed1 big garlic clove1/2 carrot, peeled and chopped1 white mushroom1/4 cup of walnuts1/2 teaspoon coriander1/2 teaspoon cumin1 teaspoon of turmeric, or to tasteSalt and pepper to tasteHeat up skillet, then add olive oil to cover pan generously. Meanwhile, cut up vegetables. Wait till oil heats up, then add onion and garlic. Simmer for 5 minutes (can cook longer if desired to roast). Add sweet potato and carrot and cook for 7 minutes, or until sweet potato is tender. Add walnuts and cook for another 3 minutes. Add mushroom and cook for 3 minutes, stirring. Add spices and cook for additional 2 minutes, stirring.Eat as side dish or on top of chicken or faux chicken.
