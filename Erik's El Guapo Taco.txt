Source: Erik
Servings/Yield: 3
Prep Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients: 
	8 corn tortillas
	12 ounces butter beans
	1 cup rice
	1 cup lettuce
	hot sauce, to taste
	1/2 cup  cheddar cheese
	1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions: 
	Chop lettuce.
	
	Start rice per rice instructions. Halfway through cooking heat up butter beans in can juices. Preheat skillet at medium heat. 
	
	When beans are ready pour olive oil in skillet and place corn tortilla with a pinch of cheddar cheese. When cheese has melted remove from skillet and add another tortilla.
