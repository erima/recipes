Crushed Tomato Sauce
=======================
Source: Peter Reinhart
Servings/Yield: 
Prep Time: 10 minutes


Ingredients
=======================

28 ounces crushed tomatoes
1/4 teaspoon black pepper
1 teaspoon dried basil
1 teaspoon dried oregano
1 tablespoon garlic powder
2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
1 teaspoon salt, to taste


Directions
=======================

IN A BOWL

Stir together all the ingredients, starting with 1/2 teaspoon salt and adding more to taste. Store in a tightly covered container in the refrigerator for up to one [week](http://bummer.com).


Notes
=======================

* substitute dried basil for 2 tablespoons of minced fresh basil

* substitute dried oregano for 1 tablespoon minced fresh oregano

* substitute dried garlic powder for 5 cloves fresh garlic, minced or pressed

* substitute red wine vinegar for lemon juice or combination of both
