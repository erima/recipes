Baked Ziti
=============================
Source: Mariello Family
Servings/Yield: 2 9x13 trays
Prep Time: 2.5 hours

=============================
Ingredients

3 boxes of spears
2 lbs ricotta
2 lbs mozz grated
3 large sauce cans
.25 lb grated parmesan or romano

=============================
Directions

Bake at 350 for 2-2.5 hours

Put layer of wax paper on, then tinfoil on top of that.
