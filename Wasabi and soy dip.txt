Source: 400 Wok & Stir Fry Recipes
Servings/Yield: 4 servings
Prep Time: 

Ingredients: 
	175ml soy sauce or shoyu
	10ml wasabi paste
	2 scallions (spring onions), diagonally sliced
	1 fresh red chili, seeded and thinly sliced into rings
	
Directions: 
	Mix the soy sauce with the wasabi paste in a bowl. Float the scallion slices on top. The chili slices can be added or left out, depending on how spicy you want the dip to be. Top with just one or two rings for color.
