TFW = TW Ö TP
Total Flour Weight equals Total Weight divided by Total Percentage

IW = IP x TFW
Ingredient Weight equals Ingredient Percentage times Total Flour Weight

IP = IW Ö TFW x 100
Ingredient Percentage equals Ingredient Weight divided by Total Flour Weight times 100
