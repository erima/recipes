Hot and Sour Soup

Source: Fresh from the Vegetarian Slow Cooker
Servings/Yield: 4
Prep Time: 40 minutes prep, 6 - 8 hours cooking (note: presumes pre-made seitan and vegetable broth)
Slow Cooker Size: 4 quart

Ingredients: 
	1/3 cup dried shiitake or cloud ear mushrooms
	4 ounces seitan, cut into thin slivers
	1 (6-ounce) can bamboo shoots, drained, rinsed, and cut into thin strips
	2 garlic cloves, minced
	1 tablespoon peeled and minced fresh ginger
	4 cups vegetable stock
	2 tablespoons rice vinegar
	2 tablespoons tamari or other soy sauce
	1 teaspoon Asian chile paste
	Salt and freshly ground black pepper
	1/2 cup frozed peas, thawed
	3 tablespoons minced scallions
	1 tablespoon toasted sesame oil
	
Directions: 
	Soak the dried mushrooms in a bowl of hot water for 20 minutes to soften. Drain, cut into thin strips, and place in a 4-quart slow cooker. Add the seitan, bamboo shoots, garlic, ginger, stock, vinegar, tamari, and chili paste. Season with salt and pepper, cover, and cook on low for 6 to 8 hours.
	
	Just before serving, stir in the peas, scallions, and sesame oil. Serve Hot.
