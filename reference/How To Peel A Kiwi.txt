1. Slice about 1/4 inch from each end of the fruit.
2. Gently press a thin-lipped teaspoon in between the skin and the flesh of the fruit.
3. Letting the kiwi rest in the palm of your hand, carefully rotate the spoon around the fruit, loosening the skin.
4. Slip the skin off in one piece.
5. Slice or dice the fruit as desired.
