Source: 101 Things To Do With A Slow Cooker
Servings/Yield: 6
Prep Time: 30 minutes prep time, 2-6 hours cooking

Ingredients: 
- 2 lbs. fako steako, chopped
- 1 large onion, chopped
- 28 oz. diced tomatoes, with liquid
- 16 oz. pureed tomatoes
- 16 oz. kidney beans, with liquid
- 4 oz. diced green chiles, with liquid
- 1 cup water
- 1 tsp. garlic, minced
- 2 tbsp. chili powder
- 2 tsp. salt
- 1 tsp. pepper


Directions: 
Combine all ingredients in greased 4 1/2 to 6 qt. slow cooker. Cover and cook on high heat 2-3 hours or low heat 4-6 hours. Serve in bread bowls. Makes 6-8 servings.
