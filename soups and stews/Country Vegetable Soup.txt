Country Vegetable Soup
============================

Source: 
Servings/Yield: 
Prep Time: 

============================

Ingredients

* 1 tablespoon olive oil
* 2 tablespoons butter
* 2 red onions
* 4 carrots, sliced
* 4 celery, sliced
* 2 leeks, sliced
* 1  potato, cut into small cubes
* 1  parnsip, cut into small cubes
* 1 clove garlic, minced
* 3 3/4 cups vegetable broth
* 1 1/4 cups soymilk
* 4 tablespoons cornstarch
* salt, to taste
* pepper, to taste
* fresh parsley, to taste
* broccoli, optional
* beans, optional
* red bell pepper, optional

============================

Directions

Heat oil and butter in a large pan and add onions, carrots, and celery. 
	
Cook over medium heat 5-10 minutes until soft. Add leaks, potato, parsnips, and garlic. Add stock to the pan, bring to a boil, and then reduce the heat and cover the pan. Simmer for 20-30 minutes until vegetables are soft. 
	
Stir milk into the cornstarch and make into a smooth paste. Add other colorful vegetables (peas, corn, broccoli) and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Add salt, pepper, and parsley. Serve hot.

=============================

Notes

Doesn't make a full pot of soup. Doubling most ingredients works well.
